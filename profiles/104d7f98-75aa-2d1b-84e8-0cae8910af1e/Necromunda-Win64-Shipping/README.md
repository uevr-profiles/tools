## Description

Howdy folks, late to the party here. This is my Necromunda 6DoF profile.  I took Markmon's profile and just added the motion controls/hand aiming. Sorry if it's redundant, I made it because I didn't see a 6DoF profile on UEVR-Profiles.com.

Some notes:
-I went with invisible arms/hands because I can't find a way to isolate them (the character always holds guns with both hands). Someone with more UEVR experience can probably find a way.
-Strength Booster (melee mode) is a little wonky but works if you get close enough (still have to pull the trigger to melee). 
-All of the other abilities have been working for me without issue. 
-I see people mentioning issues with warping in 6DoF above. That's definitely the case to an extent, but you won't be staring at your gun while running around and taking out mobs in this so speaking for myself I haven't found it to be a problem. You won't be aiming with sights in this profile, but you can get laser sights on your guns in the game and that helps.
-For me the intro cinematic locks me out of the UEVR injector if I don't start it fast enough, so I recommend getting rid of the intro cinematic with this mod: https://www.nexusmods.com/necromundahiredgun/mods/10
-There is an interesting (thankfully not problematic) issue with weapon swapping where the weapon acessories of the previous weapon duplicate and float in the air where you were standing. It doesn't seem to have any affect on your equipped weapons or accessories, just leaves some floating junk behind. Thankfully you'll be moving around alot so it's not very noticeable. 
-Make sure to press forward on the joystick when using the grapling hook, I don't remember if I had to do that in flat but it took me a bit to figure it out in 6DoF.
