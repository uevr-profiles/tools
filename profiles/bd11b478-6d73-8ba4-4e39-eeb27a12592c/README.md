## Description

Experimental 1st person Tank controls  WIP 
!!Tank dont rotate with HMD so have to rotate your self !! (profile best played in gaming chair)

Lstick Walk Forwarded and Backward RStick turn pawn left and right
Back= toggle 1st\3rd person (can be change to any button in Script UI)
L3=   sprint (sprint\walk can be adjusted in Script UI) 

- 1st person mainly use to walk around explore not for combat,dig,climbing etc...
- In combat you and switch back to 1st person and still walk around, not recommended
- Remember this is not made for 1st person so some areas that look incomplete
- In 1st person you can look though some walls to see enemy's
