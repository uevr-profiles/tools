## Description

1st Person Mod
(Place in your game pak folder : "EARTH DEFENSE FORCE IRON RAIN\EDFIR\Content\Paks")
- All characters (If you want to play with the head visible simply move the camera forwards a slight (for sprinting))
- All vehicles including DLC (remain in 3rd person but moved camera much closer)
- Some weapons clip as they are sooo BIG, un-avoidable.
- All sniper scopes (Still tricky to use, line the tip of the gun up with the top of the bottom thicker part of the crosshair *using RIGHT eye*)
- Includes a resolution fix, for 4k (3840x2160) select 640x480 in game settings

I have also attached my profile, you can use it if you want, or simply configure your own.
You may need to move the camera for personal taste.
- Head invisible by default (END key to make it visible during cut-scenes that contain the player 3D model)
- Includes file "user_script.txt" which fixes ResolutionQuality, MotionBlur, Anisotropy (and a few others). Feel free to remove or edit.

For those that wish to move the camera to a position that suits you but are having issues with the main camera slider, ..
- Bring up the UEVR menu
- Hold in your Game-Pad Right Trigger
- Use the Thumb-sticks to move the camera (much easier)
