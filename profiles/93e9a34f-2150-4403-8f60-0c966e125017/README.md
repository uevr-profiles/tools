## Description

- Regular 1st person camera attached to pawn and movement that feels just like any regular 1st person VR game (except for strafing)
- Toggle left thumb stick to run
- Quick slots moved to dpad
- Functional arms with 6dof controller tracking
- Simple Gesture based melee by swinging your arm (clicking Right trigger will also work - if you don't want to use a gesture/have tight play space)
- Accessibility options - (chief among them is the "Override difficulty" see here )- accessible in UEVR UI (click both thumbsticks) - under new tab called "Tormented Souls 2 VR"
- Custom support for all the game mechanics
- Significantly reduced pop in
- Quick recenter using both grab buttons at once
- Experimental free aim (no lock-on) option for 6dof aiming and shooting
- Optional 6Dof-less mode (aka the "jiggle" mode) - also playable with a gamepad

Installation guide:
- "Import Config" in latest UEVR nightly, then browse to the zip and click it
- In in game options - controls, change "Left stick tank controls" to "on"
- Also in in game options - controls, change "Button layout" to "type C"

Known issues:
- The bookmarks on documents/maps are blacked out
- Due to an issue with the character stuck in an elevator, the profile will switch to 3rd person fixed cameras for the "Bunker Elevator" room. (Also - make sure to unequip and equip current weapon when entering this room to fix the angle the character is holding it)
- The character's self shadow will show an extra pair of arms when looking at some angles and in certain lighting conditions
- Body mesh may glitch a little when getting off ladders
