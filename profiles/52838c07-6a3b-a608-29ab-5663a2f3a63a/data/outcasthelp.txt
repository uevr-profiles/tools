This mod has several configurable options. They are included here.
The mod ships with recommended defaults.

The gestures section. 
This provides melee swing from left to right for melee. It then
disables the X button for melee. 
The shield is used by holding the shield up to your head. Your
hand should be with your fingers pointing to the ceiling.
You can move the shield around with your left hand once it's out.

The laser aim.
This makes the game very easy so it is by default on a timer.
This means you can use it until that time expires and then
it has to regenerate to use it again. You can adjust the
time to use and regeneration factor. But 5-7 seconds on and
25%-33% seems to be good defaults.

The laser will go from green to red as the timer gets low.
