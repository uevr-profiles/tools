## Description

**Seated  6DOF Weapon Profile**

• Head aiming when no gun is present
• Right arm aiming when the gun is present
• Gun attached to the right hand when the gun is present
•Right joystick turning only
•Snap turning has not been tested 

If the gun alignment is ever off after interactions, use the **right joystick** to bring it back into place.

Includes a fix for interactions sometimes randomly not working and requiring a restart.

** Very important for this profile: If interactions do not work, simply bring up the weapon crosshair and move it down with your right arm.**

**If you ever decide to play standing, you need to switch profiles**
