## Description

             **CRONOS 1ST PERSON PROFILE -(ALPHA)**

PLEASE NOTE THIS IS STILL IN AN EARLY STATE, THERE MAY BE ISSUES

-Includes elliotttate's wonderful Cutscene Detector

**What to expect**

-Plays as close to a native 1st person shooter as it would allow w gamepad, while keeping the character body/arms visible
-Cutscenes and transitions work near perfect, camera defaults to normal view then back to 1st person
-**Main menu background will appear doubled, this is normal!**

**Known Issues**

-Camera is actually slightly in front of the character head. It's so close you can't really tell when moving/rotating, but when looking down the helmet may become slightly visible at times
-**Aiming is most accurate at closer to medium range**, far shots will land to the left of crosshair

Make sure to readjust/recalibrate your position at times to keep it as accurate as possible
