## Description

Latest preset for Quest headsets.

If you see your ball attached to your hand, just throw your Pal out once and it will go away. Weird bug

How to ride & see your mounts:
- Ride your mount, then go to Acknowledged Camera Object > Turn on Visible. You only need to do this once per session.

If your mount is a flying type:
- Press X on your keyboard to change camera. When you're back on Land, press Z on your keyboard
