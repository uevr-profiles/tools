## Description

This profile should allow you to show the cockpit whilst in the actual bumper view cam that's in game, so now you will tilt with the ship and not keep clipping out of it like you do with the other method... as soon as you inject into the game and the UEVR menu appears, tick the advanced options tab and go down to where it says main and just click on it and then you can close the menu, if you don't the cockpit view will not appear in game, I don't know why you need to do that but you only have to do it once per game session, you might need to click between first and third person in the race before the cockpit view appears, you can then adjust it to your liking in the UEVR menu by holding R trigger and moving your sticks if using a controller... I think this method is better whichever way you want to position the camera because you actually tilt with the ship and the camera always stays behind you, a lot of peoples complaints about this game was how the ship seemed to rotate on a axis and the camera didn't stay behind you... don't forget to press on the MAIN though in the advanced menu first though or it won't work, I hope somebody knows how to fix that, but it isn't a big issue anyway really.. You can open the UEVR menu and click on MAIN in the advanced options at any time, it doesn't have to be as soon as you inject.                                                                                                                   Edit - Just tick on - UObjectHook_EnabledAtStartup - and then you don't need to click on MAIN after injecting into the game.
