## Description

You have to be on the latest nightly build of UEVR.

- Switches to First person in world exploration, and back to third person in battles.
- Manual switch by @Wyermsu Auto LUA Script is by @hookman 
- First person mesh removal profile by @GingasVR , has body slightly funky. Only works on your main character, don't switch characters. 
- I attached the camera to Head based movement so your body turns with you IRL
- Removed Dpad shifting, thumbrest was annoying. To use dpad, use head gesture with left hand
- Dpad Left to switch back to Third Person in Exploration mode

Two profiles are available:
1) Performance profile, no lumen. Performs a bit better, but looks funky because there's no lumen, cvars by @markmon
2) Quality profile, with lumen. Looks better but needs very high PC to support. cvars by @Miha | Overinflated ego
