## Description

For those who are trying to enjoy a MKB play of Palworld, I made a profile that is meant for 1st person VR while still playing with mouse and keyboard (or controller)
It attaches you in the head of the player, and disables the head and hair. You can still see your body and all of the motions that your player goes through while keeping you centered on their head.
