## Description

1st person 3dof

-Attach most weapon to right controller
-Swing Right controller to Melee weapon (like pressing Rtrigger) Swing sensitivity can be adjusted in the script folder of profile edit the RightgesturebasedMelee.lua "local SWING_SPEED = 5.5     -- speed threshold" (1 through 10 1 is less effort to swing)
-Swap B and R3 (R3 is Dash B is ping location)
-Press Left controller menu button to exit any menu (cant press B)
-Some weapons are resize and offsets and be adjusted to your liking (WIP)
-Hold LB+X to toggle 1st\3rd person (loading and cutscenes)
