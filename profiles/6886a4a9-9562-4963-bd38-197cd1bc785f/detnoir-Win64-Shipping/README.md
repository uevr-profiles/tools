## Description

Right Controller Aiming Profile

Not great with UEVR, as the camera perspective occasionally gets locked by the game. In such cases: quit the game, reload, and inject again! You should be free to look and move again.
It's best to play the beginning in flat mode until you've scanned the first victim with the Reconstructor (tutorial), then switch to VR. However it's also good to play in VR, including the usage of the tools. 

- Invisible body/arms
- Aiming with the right controller (Input -> Modify Player Control Rotation)
- 3DOF
- Slightly sharpened
