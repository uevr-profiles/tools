## Controls
### 1.    Right Controller:
⦁    A button: Pick up Items
⦁    B Button: Throw
⦁    Trigger: FireGuns
⦁    Grip: Grab Enemies
⦁    Grip over Right SHoulder: Swap to Secondary/Primary
⦁    Grip Right Hip: Swap to Primary
⦁    RStick Up: Kick (hmd direction)
⦁    RStick Down: Dodge
### 2. Left Controller:
⦁    X button: xbox B button
⦁    Y button: xbox Y button(reload)
⦁    Grip Mag area short: reload
⦁    Grip Mag area long: Special
⦁    Grip Enemy: Grab Enemies
⦁    Grabbed Enemies+ Swing: perform execution(doesn't kill and needs them to be half hp)
⦁    Trigger: while touching an enemy during their attack will perform a Counter
